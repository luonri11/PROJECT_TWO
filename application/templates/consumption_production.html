Summary

- Only Tasmania saw increased energy use while energy use fell in all other states. This growth was spread across sectors, with residential, manufacturing, agriculture and transport consumption all increasing. Victoria and NSW has recorded a decline in energy consumption. Energy consumption in the Electricity supply sector fell in all states due to the replacement of thermal generation sources such as coal and gas with non-thermal renewables, providing about the same level of national total generation.
